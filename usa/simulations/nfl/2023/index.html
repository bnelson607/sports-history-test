Tiebreakers

  • Buffalo won the AFC East title over Miami by virtue of a head-to-head sweep during the regular season.
  • Cleveland earned the 6-seed over Miami by finishing with a better conference record (8-4 vs. 7-5).
  • San Francisco earned the 1-seed over Dallas and Detroit based on having a better conference record.
  • Dallas earned the 2-seed over Detroit from their head-to-head victory in Week 17.
  • Green Bay earned the 7-seed over Seattle by having a better strength of victory (.458 vs. .392) and over New Orleans by having a better conference record (7-5 vs. 6-6).
